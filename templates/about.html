Welcome to Lango!

Lango is a free offline travel tool that will quickly give you access to common travel phrases in many different languages. Lango allows you to view common phrases for many different settings such as restaurants, museums, airports, tourism, ect... and allows you to switch between languages with ease. Lango does not require an internet connection, which can be difficult to access while traveling abroad.
Current available languages
Chinese, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Russian, Spanish, Turkish
